When Geo Met Kirsten

A Story Worth Writing

"Some chapters are written by destiny itself."

Chapter 1: The First Game

It all started on a random November night, the kind you’d think would be completely ordinary. Geo sat in his room, logging into Mobile Legends, expecting nothing more than a usual match. But fate had other plans. Among the players was Kirsten, her name popping up on the screen like it was meant to catch his attention.

She wasn’t just good; she was fun to play with. Every word she typed in the chat box made him laugh. She had this way of making even losing feel okay, like the game wasn’t about winning anymore, but just having fun. For some reason, Geo felt a little spark that night. It wasn’t something grand, but it was enough to make him think, Maybe this person is special.

Chapter 2: Late Nights and Laughter

What started with gaming soon turned into longer conversations. At first, it was just about the game, strategies, characters, wins, and losses. But as the days went by, they began talking about life. Their chats stretched into the late hours, sometimes even until sunrise.

“Kirsten,” Geo said one night, “bakit parang ang gaan mong kausap? Parang hindi ako nauubusan ng kwento sa’yo.”

Kirsten laughed softly. “Siguro kasi hindi kita ini-stress. Ako din naman, Geo. Ang dali mong pasayahin, ang dali mong mahalin.”

Chapter 3: Netflix Nights

Then came the movie nights. Kirsten bought an account for the both of them which cost ₱249. They started watching Netflix together—one of them would pick a movie, and they’d stream it at the same time. But sometimes, the movies became background noise.

“Baby,” Kirsten teased one night, “hindi mo na pinapansin yung movie. Ang dami mo nang kwento!”

Geo grinned. “Eh kasi ikaw, mas interesting ka kaysa sa movie.”
